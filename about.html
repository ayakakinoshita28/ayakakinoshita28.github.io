“Adrian Khalif”

The Artist

Adrian Khalif Akbar Thamrin or known as Adrian Khalif (born 24 June 1992) is a singer and rapper from Indonesia.

Early life

Raised in a family of diplomats, Adrian was used to living on the move from one country to another. He spent his teenage years in New York City where he became interested in hip hop. After studying French Literature at the Faculty of Humanities, University of Indonesia, he signed a recording contract with E-Motion Entertainment. Adrian is the nephew of famous football pundit, Hanif Thamrin.

Carrier

Adrian started his career with the release of his debut non-album single, titled Made In Jakarta, he collaborated with Dipha Barus in 2017. He released his debut album, titled Embrace, Embrace was released on November 22, 2019.
